Whiteboard

On the whiteboard you can read documents and notes, watch videos, comment on documents, and download them.

A whiteboard is not limited in size. You can move around and zoom. To move the whiteboard around just drag it with the mouse. For zooming use the mousewheel or the zoom buttons.

Use the buttons for viewing the full whiteboard or return to the initial view.

You can search the whiteboard content by typing into the search field.

To download a document hover it with the mouse, the Download button appears in the document's lower left corner. When hovered also the Fullscreen button appears. Use it for comfortable reading.

To see the document's discussion press the bubble button. The discussion is displayed in the right panel then.

Editors can put new items on the whiteboard and upload documents. This is the purpose of the Add button in the whiteboard's upper left corner. It is appears only if you're an editor.
