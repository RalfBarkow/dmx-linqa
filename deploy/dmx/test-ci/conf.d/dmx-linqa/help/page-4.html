Discussion

Besides the whiteboard every workspace has the chat/discussion panel. You can adjust the size of the discussion in relation to the whiteboard. You can close the discussion panel completely to give full room to the whiteboard.

Messages and their translations are displayed at the same time. The user's original message is displayed in left column, the translation in right column. It is indicated whether the translation was created automatically, or edited manually.

Messages are displayed with the newest on top. For each message its date and author are shown. When placing the mouse cursor on the author her email address will be displayed, provided she had agreed so in her user profile.

Write your message in the input field at the bottom. You can format your message by doing a text selection. A menu is appearing then. You can create weblinks and include images. You can attach arbitrary files to your message (paperclip button), downloadable by other workspace members later on. To send your message click the Send button (paper airplane).

You can relate your message to another message. You can edit messages written by you later on. You can delete your messages.

There is both, a general chat and discussion targeted at specific documents or textblocks.
