Welcome to Zukunftswerk platform!

The platform helps with overcoming language barriers by providing save shared workspaces. A workspace comprises of both, a whiteboard on which texts, documents, videos, notes, and photos can be placed, and a chat/discussion panel.

The contents of both, the whiteboard, and all chat/discussion messages are translated automatically. So everything happening on the platform is available in German and French instantly.

Use the upper right button to choose your preferred display language.
