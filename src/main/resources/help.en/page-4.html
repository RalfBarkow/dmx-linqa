Workspaces

As a user of the Linqa platform you are a member of one ore more shared workspaces. A workspace is a topic specific area where you communicate and collaborate with the other workspace members.

A workspace is a safe place. Only members of a workspace can see what is going on inside. In the Linqa platform there are no public workspaces. The Linqa platform is invitation-only.

To see the workspaces you're a member of and jump between them use the menu at the top of the screen.

Workspaces are created and invitations are send out by the platform administrators.
